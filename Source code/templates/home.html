        Traffic congestion and delays at toll plazas are a persistent problem.
        The project has been designed for the automation of toll tax payments 
        by calculating the distance travelled by the vehicle.This project aims 
        at designing a system, which automatically identifies the vehicle when 
        it enters the Toll-Zone and initiates the system to process the 
        distance travelled and the automated deduction of Toll tax.
